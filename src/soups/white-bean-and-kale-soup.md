# White Bean and Kale Soup

from:

## Ingredients

- 1 yellow or sweet onion
- 3 celery ribs
- 3 carrots
- 6 cloves Garlic
- 2 cans cannellini beans
- 1 yukon gold potato
- olive oil
- Red pepper flakes
- artichoke hearts (chopped)
- 3 1/2 to 4 cups vegetable broth

### Bouquet Garni

- rosemary
- sage
- bay leaves

### Gremmalata

- parsely
- garlic
- lemon zest
- Sea salt
